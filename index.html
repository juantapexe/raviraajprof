Ravi Raj Creations is a Mumbai based film production company, passionate about impacting lives, shaping the thinking process and perception of the present and future generations, through gripping storytelling, striking visuals, and innovative design.

The company covers a diverse area of film making

including daily soaps, drama series, religious shows, TV commercials, promotional films, short films, and feature films.

Ravi Raj Creations has now made a concentrated push in creating its mark in the film industry and also on the OTT platforms, through web series.

F O U N D E R


Mr. Raviraaj K Mahato has risen from a very simple background, nowhere remotely connected to the film or television industry. An obedient yet persistent child, he finally persuaded his humble parents to let him travel to Mumbai to pursue his passion.

Starting as an intern, he worked his way up through sheer determination, dedication and hard work. And now, his career as Director Producer, spanning over 4 decades, boasts of many of the most well known serials in the history of our television industry.

Since 2017, the company has produced several hugely successful drama series.


AGNIPHERA

500 EPISODES AIRED ON ‘&TV’ AND ‘ZEE5'


Agnifera was one of the most-watched shows on ‘&TV’ and ‘Zee TV’! It was a fan favourite and accounted for a lot of TRP.

Based on a story of two brides Ragini and Srishti, who both love Anurag and want to marry him. According to Zee5 “The show's success and the awards won by the cast as well as the crew could be due to the unique and interesting storyline. The show caught the audience's interest and spiked curiosity.”


LAAL ISHQ

51 EPISODES AIRED ON ‘&TV’


A strange and creative mix of romance and horror! And we thought the two weren't blendable!

This show aired on ‘&TV’ has passionate love stories with nail biting, spooky, supernatural twists. This intertwining of romance and thrill, portrays love in unenexpected colours, highlighting its eternal and obsessive aspect, keeping the viewers spookily thrilled, and ever-glued to the screens.


21 EPISODES AIRED ON ‘& TV’


Mauka-E-Vardaat is a show which presented extraordinary tales of impossible crimes, which challenge the viewers’ imagination, leaving them speechless. A riveting weekday series, based on the world of extreme crime, hitting upon us with the impactful 'Woman Power'. Here we see, a woman protagonist instrumental in unravelling the mystery to extraordinarily heinous crimes. In this series we see an anthology of stories which put the spotlight on the victims of these heinous crimes, who are often faced with the dilemma to choose between that which is ethical and inethical.
